Tea

Description

This is a very simple recipe which is a must have in your Beverage Menu

This easy recipe should always be prepared for the Guest

Ingredients

  • Teapot
  • Teacups
  • Water
  • Tea leafs
  • Milk(optional)
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil the water(90ml)
  2. Add the Tea leafs(2-5gm) on the water
  3. Add the milk(optional 10ml)after the tealeafs starts affecting
  4. Add the sugar(1teaspoon)
  5. Enjoy!
